Homestar Runner animation site was all the rage back in 2003. I even blogged about it. Five years later, my The Cheat T-shirt is still in the wardrobe rotation. I even stop by the site occasionally to see what's new. It's impressively meta and self-referential, with many of the pieces "created" by the characters themselves.

I always make a point to stop in after Halloween to listen to Strong Bad ridicule costumes based on the site. The latest installment is good. It's funnier if you can follow all the characters and references, dutifully recorded this episode's page in the wiki, but you may find it fun just to hear Strong Bad go at it.

If you like this one, check out the 2003, 2005, 2006. and 2007 editions.
